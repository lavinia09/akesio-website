Meet our team

We aim to reinvent primary care with a data-driven preventative approach, helping individuals to take care of their health in a more scientific way. We are a team with a groundbreaking approach to medicine, dedicated to prevention and health.

Dr. Lavinia Ionita

CEO

Dr. Ionita graduated from the Craiova Faculty of Medicine, and the American Academy of Anti-Aging Medicine. She specialised in Addictology at the University of Paris-V and holds a Master’s Degree in Clinical Psychology and Psychopathology from the University of Paris-VIII. She also holds a Certificate in Genetics & Genomics from the University of Stanford with minors in Genetic Engineering, Biotechnology, Genomics Approach of Cancers, Stem Cells & Pharmacogenomics.

Dr. Monica Lascar

CMO

Dr Lascar trained in sexual health and HIV at University College Hospital, holds a research degree from UCL in viral immunology, and has worked as a consultant for the NHS since 2010. She brings a holistic approach to her care for patients and has trained with the Institute of Functional Medicine. She has treated patients with a variety of complex problems, including fatigue, immune dysfunction, perimenopause and other hormonal problems.

Georgiana Petria

CTO

Georgiana has extensive experience as a software engineer, having built products for millions of users at Amazon, Amadeus, and Murex. She was a co-founder and CTO of a medTech company using computer vision to remotely monitor patients with Parkinson's disease. She holds a master's of engineering from Ensimag and has done research in data mining at Supelec. Georgiana is on a mission to use tech to disrupt tranditional medicine.
